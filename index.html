Government Initiatives to Prevent Cyber Fraud

India has launched several strategic initiatives to safeguard citizens from cyber threats and enhance national cyber defense.

Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (I4C)

Set up under the Ministry of Home Affairs to tackle cybercrime across the country through a coordinated and multi-disciplinary approach.

Cyber Swachhta Kendra

A Botnet Cleaning and Malware Analysis Center initiated by CERT-In to help internet users clean their devices from malware and viruses.

National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal

Citizens can report cybercrimes like online financial fraud and cyberbullying via cybercrime.gov.in.

Digital India Programme

Alongside promoting digital infrastructure, the initiative focuses on spreading cyber hygiene awareness and responsible online behavior.
